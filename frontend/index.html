Brief Introduction

Cardinal's SpotN'Fix is a web-based reporting and monitoring system for faulty university equipment. It allows students, faculty, and staff to report faulty university equipment and facilities while enabling maintenance personnel to efficiently manage, monitor, and resolve the reported issues.

Key Features

  • Seamless facility issue reporting
  • Real-time status tracking
  • Maintenance monitoring dashboard
  • Organized repair records
  • Faster response to campus concerns
