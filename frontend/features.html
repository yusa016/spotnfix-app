Features

Issue Reporting

Students and faculty can report faulty facilities and equipment by providing details and photo evidence.

Issue Tracking

Users can monitor the status of their submitted reports, from pending review to resolution.

Maintenance Management

Maintenance personnel can assign, update, and manage repair tasks efficiently.

Search and Filtering

Reports can be searched and filtered by building, issue type, status, or date.

Report Generation

Administrators can generate summaries and maintenance reports for monitoring purposes.
