## Brief Description

Cardinal’s SpotN’Fix is a web-based reporting and monitoring system developed to address problems encountered when reporting faulty university equipment and facility maintenance needs. It provides a centralized platform where students, faculty, and staff can report any maintenance problem without the need to go through complicated procedures that would delay the process.

The system helps maintenance workers manage the process of receiving reports and monitoring the progress of resolving the issues raised by the users. This system will enable effective communication and increase accountability by ensuring that the maintenance staff is aware of any needed or ongoing maintenance activities within the university.

Thus, Cardinal’s SpotN’Fix contributes to a safer, productive, more functional, and better-maintained campus environment for all users.
