About Cardinal's SpotN'Fix

Cardinal's SpotN'Fix was developed to manage maintenance concerns within the Mapua university. Manual reporting methods can result in delayed responses and unorganized records, leading to prolonged facility downtime and potential safety risks.

The system provides a centralized platform where people of the Mapua community can submit reports regarding damaged facilities and equipment. These reports are then organized and monitored by maintenance personnel to ensure timely action and resolution.

Mission

To provide an efficient, transparent, and accessible platform for reporting and monitoring campus facility concerns, to promote a safer and more conducive learning environment for all Mapuans.

Vision

To become the university's trusted solution for facility maintenance management, ensuring that campus resources remain safe, functional, and well-maintained.

Purpose

The project aims to streamline the reporting and maintenance process of university facilities and equipment by establishing a centralized web-based system. The system then communicates between the Mapua community and maintenance personnel, allowing concerns to be addressed more effectively.

Objectives

  • Enable students, faculty, and staff to easily report facility-related issues.
  • Provide maintenance personnel with organized and actionable system tracking.
  • Improve response time for repair and maintenance requests.
  • Prevent further damage to university assets through early issue detection.
  • Enhance campus safety and overall facility management efficiency.
